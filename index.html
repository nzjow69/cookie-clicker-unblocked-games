What Is Cookie Clicker?

For the uninitiated, Cookie Clicker is a simple yet addictive incremental game where the goal is to produce as many cookies as possible.

How It Works:

  1. Click the Cookie: Each click earns you one cookie.
  2. Upgrade Your Production: Use cookies to buy upgrades, such as automatic cookie producers like grandmas, farms, and even cookie factories.
  3. Scale Up: The more you upgrade, the more cookies you earn passively.
  4. Prestige and Repeat: Once you’ve reached a certain level, you can reset and earn special bonuses to start the cookie empire all over again.

It’s simple, satisfying, and strangely addictive.
